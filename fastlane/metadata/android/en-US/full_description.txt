Fat Loss is a lightweight open-source Android fitness tracker focused on daily fat-loss habits.

It helps you track the basics that matter: workouts, walking, food, sleep, weight progress, steps, reminders, and simple analytics. The app is built natively with Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Room, and Material 3.

Features:

- Daily dashboard for walk, workout, diet, and sleep targets.
- Editable weekly workout plan with custom exercises.
- Food log with calorie and protein tracking.
- Weight, photo, sleep, and step progress tracking.
- Health Connect and device step counter support where available.
- Reminder and alarm tools for workouts, meals, and habit prompts.
- JSON and CSV backup export, import, and shareable backup text.
- Editable profile details including name, age, gender, height, current weight, and target weight.
